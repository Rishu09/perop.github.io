About


I was born in Yokohama, Japan....

I really love taking a picture, writing and cooking.

I majored in Game Theory and Advertising Communication in college. My love for photography led me to join Mindpalette Inc, a creative camera application development company based in Tokyo, as an intern.

After graduation, I spent some time as an account manager for a social media advertising firm and later become a community manager of a camera app ”Snapeee.”

And recently, I've become interested in design and started to learn HTML, CSS, and Photoshop.

So I built this site - where I share the things I love, the things I’m learning and the things I find incredible. Hopefully you’ll get to learn something new too:)

By the way, I sometimes use "perop" for my social accounts. It doesn't mean much, just a random sequence of characters I came up with.

I'm just having a lot of fun exploring!

Reverse Rock, Paper, MYO

Develop / Graphic / UI Design Work

Won the Prize at Y Combinator Hackathon

iOS Game allowing you to use MYO to control hand movement gestures to play a game of "Rock, Paper, Scissors" with a twist. Instead of trying to win the game based on the original strategy, the objective is to purposely lose to gain points, within the time frame set.



Key Features

・Use of MYO gesture armband and playable using the buttons at the bottom

・iOS game device

・Novel remake of the original "Rock, Paper, Scissors" game

・Timer bar
