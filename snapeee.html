Snapeee

A photo sharing app 2015

Snapeee has over a million girls and women users all over the world.

It's said the replacement of Instagram for asian countries.

After logging in the app, you can choose favorite images from your library or

take new ones and then start editing with cute stickers and beautiful filters.


Client: MindPalette Inc.

My role: UX, Creative Direction, User and Customer Acquisition & Monetization

Options are extremely varied here.

From traditional filters like what you'd find in Instagram to the

addition of stickers, labels, and captions, you can do a lot to your

images, and the interface is very easy to use.

I was responsible for creating our own persona and actively went out within the

online community to connect with potential customers and advocate the brand.
